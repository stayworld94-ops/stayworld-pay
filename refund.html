Refund & Cancellation Policy — StayWorld

1. General Policy

Guests may cancel reservations according to the cancellation terms displayed at the time of booking.

2. Refunds

  • Full refund for cancellations made within 24 hours of booking.
  • Partial refund may apply for cancellations made after 24 hours, depending on host policies.
  • No refund for no-shows.

3. Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 7–14 business days through the original payment method.

4. Contact

All refund and cancellation requests must be submitted to support@stayworldbooking.com.
